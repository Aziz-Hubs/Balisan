# The Perfect Old Fashioned

A classic cocktail that highlights the quality of your bourbon...

## Ingredients

- 2 oz Bourbon
- 1 sugar cube
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters
- Orange peel

## Instructions

1. Muddle the sugar and bitters.
2. Add ice and bourbon.
3. Stir well.
4. Garnish with an orange peel.
